🎯 Must-Know Answers (Top 5)

👉 Why perceptron fails on XOR?
Because XOR is not linearly separable.

👉 When does perceptron converge?
Only when data is linearly separable.

👉 What is perceptron loss?
Loss penalizes only misclassified points.

👉 What is perceptron trick?
Updating weights to correct misclassified points.

👉 Why linear classifier?
Because it creates a straight-line boundary.
